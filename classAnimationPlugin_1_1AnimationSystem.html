Detailed Description

The AnimationSystem is the main system for coupling TimedSystems.

On one hand, it manages the AnimationComponents, i.e. skeleton animation and display. On the other hand, it is responsible for transmitting calls to animation-related systems, for example physics systems that must play with the animation.
