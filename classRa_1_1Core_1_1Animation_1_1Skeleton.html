Detailed Description

The Skeleton handle class.

A skeleton handle is a set of joint transforms with an associated joint hierarchy, represented by a graph ( adjacency list ).

Skeleton bones represent a couple of joints: the proximal joint and the distal joint, the former begin the parent of the latter in the hierarchy. For animation purposes, a bone transform refers to the proximal joint's tranform.
